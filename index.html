About

I’m a Mechanical Engineering student in the University Honors Program at the University of Connecticut with a strong interest in robotics, automation, embedded systems, and intelligent systems. My goal is to work at the intersection of mechanical engineering, software, controls, and artificial intelligence to design systems that improve how people live and interact with the world around them.
I am especially drawn to projects that combine hands-on engineering with analytical problem solving.
